If you are in India and only have UPI, the flow is usually not direct UPI to Monero. Most people need to move from INR to a crypto platform first, then buy or swap into Monero, and finally send Monero to the wallet shown above.

  1. Open a crypto platform that lets you add INR and supports Monero or Monero withdrawals.
  2. Add money using UPI or bank transfer.
  3. Buy Monero directly if the platform supports it.
  4. If Monero is not available, buy a supported coin and convert or swap it to Monero.
  5. Copy the Monero wallet address from this page exactly.
  6. Withdraw or send the Monero to that wallet address.
  7. Double-check the address before confirming, because crypto transfers usually cannot be reversed.

Platform support changes often. If one app does not support Monero, try another compliant option. For most people, this takes more effort than a normal UPI payment.
